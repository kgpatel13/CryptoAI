# CryptoAI Research Platform

CryptoAI v4.0 promotes the project from a production-quality paper trading platform into a quantitative research platform.

## Goals

- Preserve every important market and trading event.
- Build feature vectors for every opportunity and outcome.
- Make strategy results measurable by pair, chain, DEX, hour, and execution quality.
- Prepare for replay, backtesting, walk-forward validation, and AI ranking.

## v4.0 Components

- Feature Store
- Research Dashboard
- Mission Control dashboard page
- Decision Explainability helper
- SQLite feature table
- JSONL and CSV feature exports

## Future extensions

- Immutable event replay
- Parameter sweeps
- Walk-forward validation
- Market regime detection
- AI ranking and confidence calibration
