# CryptoAI v3.3 - Intelligent Portfolio & Risk Engine

This release upgrades CryptoAI from paper trade execution to paper portfolio management.

## Added

1. Stateful paper portfolio risk service.
2. Duplicate trade suppression by pair and direction.
3. Trade cooldown by pair and direction.
4. Dynamic paper position sizing based on cash, portfolio value, and risk percentage.
5. Paper cash accounting after simulated fills.
6. Open-position tracking in `data/paper_portfolio_state.json`.
7. Max daily paper trade guard.
8. Max open position guard.
9. Chain and token exposure limits.
10. Dashboard Paper Portfolio page.
11. Paper report portfolio summary and risk rejection reasons.
12. Unit tests for risk sizing, duplicate suppression, cooldowns, and daily trade limits.

## Why this matters

Before v3.3, repeated scheduler runs could keep filling the same paper-simulated opportunity. That was useful for validating the pipeline, but it was not realistic portfolio behavior.

v3.3 changes the system from:

```text
signal -> paper fill
```

into:

```text
signal -> strategy risk -> portfolio risk -> paper fill or risk rejection
```

This is a major step toward a real trading system because it starts managing capital, exposure, duplicate signals, and risk limits.

## Expected behavior

The first approved signal may create a simulated paper fill. Repeated identical signals within the duplicate window or cooldown should be marked `RISK_REJECTED`, not filled again.
