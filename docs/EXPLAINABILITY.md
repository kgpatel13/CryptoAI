# CryptoAI Explainability

The explainability layer exists so every important trading decision can be audited.

## Questions every decision should answer

- Was this a paper-only or live-eligible opportunity?
- Was the quote fresh and healthy?
- Did the estimated edge exceed the threshold?
- Did portfolio risk approve the action?
- Was the action blocked by duplicate exposure, cooldown, stale data, or live safety gates?

## v4.0 scope

v4.0 includes deterministic explanations for opportunity and order records. The future AI ranking layer must use the same explainability discipline and must not bypass risk controls.
