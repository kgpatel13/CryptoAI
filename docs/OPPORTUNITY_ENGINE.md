# CryptoAI Opportunity Engine

CryptoAI v2.2 introduces a normalized opportunity engine.

## Purpose

The opportunity engine sits between raw scanners and strategy/risk layers.

Raw quotes are converted into comparable opportunity candidates.

## Current scoring inputs

- Gross spread %
- Estimated cost buffer %
- Estimated net edge %
- Latency sensitivity
- Liquidity status placeholder
- Source quality

## Future scoring inputs

- Pool liquidity
- Slippage simulation
- Real gas estimate
- MEV risk
- Route complexity
- Historical success rate
- Execution probability
